How to Play

Drifting echoes carry single‑word palindromes. Type a palindrome to resolve its echo and let it bloom. Keep the garden clear of unresolved forms.

  • Click Start to begin.
  • Focus on an echo by typing its first letter.
  • Continue typing to complete the word; your progress is highlighted.
  • Resolved echoes disappear in a warm glow, adding to your score.
  • If too many echoes reach the bottom, the garden becomes overgrown.
  • Press Esc to pause or resume at any time.
