Palindromes

Words and sentences that read the same forwards and backwards. A mirror hidden in language, waiting to be noticed.

Single Words
level radar madam civic refer rotor kayak racecar redder reviver repaper deified minim stats solos tenet wow mom noon pop
The shortest palindrome? Any single letter. The longest English single-word palindrome in common use is racecar — seven letters, perfectly mirrored around the ‘e’.
